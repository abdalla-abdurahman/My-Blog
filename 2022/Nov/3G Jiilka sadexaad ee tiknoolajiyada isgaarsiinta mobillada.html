3G, Jiilka sadexaad ee tiknoolajiyadda isgaarsiinta mobillada

By ABDALLA ABDURAHMAN

21 Nov 2021

3G waa jiilka saddexaad ee tignoolajiyada isgaarsiinta mobilada. Jiilkani waxa uu ka soo daba baxay 2G oo lagu yaqaanay in uu soo kordhiyay adeegyada SMS,MMS, GPRS & EDGE oo ah adeeg saamaxayaa in mobilkaaga aad ku isticmaasho internetka oo markii ugu horaysay 2G-gu suurtagaliyay iyada oo mobillada keliya cod iyo fariimaha SMS lagu isticmaalo. Waxa la sheega in tobankii sanno-ba tiknoolaji cusub oo isgaarsiinta mobilladu in uu soo baxo.1980 waxa la soo saaray 1G, waxa ku xigay 2G 1990, ka dibna 3G oo la hirgaliyay 2000.

Sababihii keenay samaynta 3G-ga

Kobaca Internetka ee dabayaaqadii 1990-aadkii ayaa sababay in ay aad u kordho baahida loo qabo mobile internet xawaarihiisu sareeyo. Waxa kale oo wakhtigaa aad u badanayay dadka qaadanayay mobillada oo system-kii 2G gee jiray aanu qaadi karayn. Taasi waxay keentay in qorshaha ujeedka 3G-gu noqdo mid xalliya labadaa dhibaato kor ku sheegnay oo ah kow in internetkiisa xawaarihiisu sareeyo iyo laba in uu capacity-giisu (tirada dadka isticmaali kara) bato maadaama waaga qaadashada mobilladu ay aad u badanaysay. Hadaba se, xawaaraha internetka ee 2G-gu waxa uu ahaa ilaa 50kbps oo GPRS ka ah iyo 230kbps oo EDGE ka ahaa taa macnaheedu yahay hadaad doonaysid hees inaad soo dejiso waxay kugu qaadanaysay 2 daqiiqo, movie hadaad soo dejinaysana illaa 19 saacadood. Dabcan xawaarahaasi aad ayaa uu u hooseeyay, si loo kordhiyo xawaaraha internet-ka ee mobillada waxa soo baxay 3G. Isbedelka ama faa’idada ugu wayn ee 3G-gu keenay waxay ahayd internet xawaarihiisu aad uga sareeyo 2G. Celcelis xawaaraha internetka 3G waxa weeye 4Mbps, oo la macnaa in isla movie-gii hore ay kugu qaadanayso 1 saacad oo keliya in aad ku soo dejisato.

Qaabdhismeedka network-ga ee 3G-gu waxa uu aad ula mid yahay ka 2G-ga hase ahaate waxa ay ku kala duwan yihiin janallada hirarka (frequency channels) iyo Access technology-ga oo CDMA/WCDMA oo 3G gu isticmaalo una saamaxaysa capacity badan (dadbadan wada isticmalaan iyada oo aanay hawadu cuslaanin) halka 2G gu lahaa TDMA oo ka capacity yaraa. Marka system ka capacity-giisu yaryahay waxa dhaca congestion oo ah tusaale, hadii goob dad badani iskugu yimaada siiba wakhtiyada ciidaha waxa badanka la dareema in calls-ku aanay is gelin ama ay go’aan ama xataa adeega Zaadka oo kale si fiican u shaqayn ama aan fariimaha SMS ku aanay qofka hore usoo gelin, waxa la yidhaa hawada culus (congestion) oo ah dad ka badan dadkii loogu talo galay goobtaas ayaa doonaaya in hal mar ay wada isticmaalaan adeegyada shirkadda.

Faaidooyinka laga helay 3G-ga


Faa'idooyinkii uu soo kordhiyay waxa ka mid ahaa;

  • Mobile internet xawaarihiisu sareeyo oo suurto galiyay in la isku waxo video (video calling) , video conferencing, online streaming, live tv broadcasting iwm oo ku soo beegmay markay soo baxeen mobilladii touch-screen ka ahaa.
  • Cod tayadiisu aad u wanaagsan tahay.
  • Bandwidth ilaa 20MHz oo aad uga badan kii hore ee 2G-ga.
  • Capacityga tirada dadka isticmaali karaa oo ilaa 5 jibaar ka badan ka 2G-ga.

Hadaba ma laga yaaba in qofku mobile kiisa ku wada isticmaalo 2G iyo 3G ? Haa, hadii 3G loogu talogaly mobile ka oo uu backward compatibily leeyahay dabcan 2G na waa uu ugu shaqaynayaa. Hadiise loogu talo galay 2G markaa suurto gal ma aha in uu ugu shaqeeyo 3G na. Smart phones kan danbe, badanka waxay ay ku wada shaqeeyan 4G, iyaga oo leh backward compatibility 3G iyo 2G ah oo uu qofku dooran karo ama system ka mobile-ku automatic uu dooranayo taas oo ku xidhan coverage-ka uu helayo mobile-kaagu. Hadii aad joogto meel coverage ka 4G gu daciif yahay oo 3G coverage keedu aa u wanaagsan yahay waxa uu mobile-kagu automatic u dooranaya kolba ka signal kiisu xoogan yahay oo hadda ah 3G.
