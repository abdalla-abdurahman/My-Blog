Waa maxay Bitcoin sidee baanay u shaqaysaa?

By ABDALLA ABDURAHMAN

19 Nov 2021

Bitcoin waa lacag electronic ama digital oo ann lahayn waraaqo (notes) sida lacagaha kale ee caadiga ah.Waxa aad lacagtan ku shuban kartaa ama aad ku kaydsan kartaa crypto wallets oo ah Applications ku jira mobile-kaaga ama computer-kaaga. Waxa loo iibsada sida alaabta oo kale ama loo sariftaa sida lacagaha oo kale. Hadii aad lacag Dollar ah haysatid oo aad doonayso Somaliland Shilling waxa aad taktaa sariflaha (exchange) oo kaa qaata Dollarka adna ku siiya Shilling-ka inta uu marayo maalintaas (rate). Bitcoin markaad iibsanayso waxaad ka iibsanaysa Crypto Exchange oo ah sarifle kuu badalaya Dollarkii aad sidatay oo ku siinaya Bitcoin (kuugu so diraya account-kaga madama oo aanay lahayn notes) kolba inta ay marayso wakhtigaas. Bitcoin waxa laga maamulaa bitcoin network oo ah shebakad ay ku xidhiidhsanyihiin computers badan oo kala yaala meelo badan oo kaladuwan aduunyada oo dhan. Hadaba, shebekadan online ka ah cid khaasa oo maamushaa ma jirto. Computer kasta oo ku xidhiidhsan waxaa loo yaqaan Node waxaana ku jira Bitcoin Software, oo u baahan internet si uu ugu xidhiidhsamo shabakada inteeda kale. Hadii aad haysatid internet iyo computer uu ku jiro software-ka Bitcoin, waxa aad ka mid noqon karta Bitcoin Network meesha doontid adoo jooga. Nodes-kan ama computers ayaa maamula shebekadan ee shakhsi ama shirkad gaara oo maamushaa ma jirto waxaana la dhahaa Decentralised System oo aan lahayn xarun gooniya oo laga maamulo. Shaqadan ay qabtaan computers-kani waa laba, kow, in ay hubiyaan in qofku lacagta Bitcoin ee uu dirayo ay tahay lacag uu haystay markii hore oo ugu jirtay accountkiisa iyo laba in ay kaydiyaan macluumaadka la xidhiidha transaction-kaa qofku sameeyay oo la yidhaa ledger waxaana ku jira macluumaadka sida transaction Id, sending address, receiving address.

Aasaasitaankii Bitcoin

Bitcoin waxaa la ikhtiraacay 2008, hase ahaate isticmaalkeedu waxa u bilaabmay 2009. Lama garanayo xogta shakhsiyadeed ee cidda samaysay in hal qof ama ay koox yihiin. kelliya waxa la ogyahay in magaca Satoshi Nakamota cidda leh ay ka danbayso. Satoshi Nakamota waa shakhsigiii ama kooxdii qortay waraaqdii sharaxaysay qaabka ay Bitcoin u shaqaynayso sidaasaana lagu ogaaday in qof kaasi ama kooxdaasi ay ikhtiraaceen Bitcoin. Hadii aad rabto waraaqdaas halkan ka akhri.

Inta ay jirtay Bitcoin marxalado badan ayay soo martay qiimaheeduna ma ahayn mid joogto ahaa. 2009 qiimaha Bitcoin suuqa ka taagna waxa uu ahaa $0, wakhtiga maqaalkan la qorayo waxay maraysaa $16,536.77 markii ugu saraysay waxay gaadhay $68,789.63 oo ahayd 8.Nov.2021, waxaana keenaya isbed-bedlkan aadka u badan ma jirto dawlad ama central bank goynaysa qiimaheeda waxaana la yidha waa Decentralised. Qiimaheeda waxa jaan gooya kolba supply and demand-ka sida alaabaha kale ee suuqa lagu iibiyo. Tusaale, hadii shiidaalka wadanka soo galayaa uu aad u bahato (supply) waxa yaraada hoos u dhaca qiimaha (price) waayo supply-ga oo ah shiidaalka ayaa ka badan demand-ka oo ah baahida dadku u qabaan inay iibsadaan shiidalkaa. Bitcoin waa sidaas oo kale, markasta oo ay dadka iibsanaya Bitcoin bataan waxa kor u kaca qiimaheeda markasta oo ay dadku iska iibiyaana waxa hoos u dhaca qiimaheeda.

Maxaa loo aaminay Bitcoin?


Aynu eegno waxa qiimaha bitcoin ka dhigay qaali iyo sababta ay dadku u aamineen isticmaalka lacagtan. Bal is waydii sababta U.S. Dollar-ku qaali u yahay meel kastana looga isticmaalo halka SL Shilling-kuna bilaa qiimo uu yahay xataa wadanka gudihiisana aan aad looga isticmaalin. Astaamaha uu leeyahay Dollarku-ku ayaa ka dhigay mid awood leh. Waxa ka mida in dawladda ugu dhaqaalaha badan ay soo saarto Dollar-ka, in wadankani siyaasad ahaan iyo dhaqaale ahaan uu aad u xasilloon yahay (hadii dagaalo ka dhacaan ama qalalaase siyaasadeed Dollarka awoodiisu wuu yaranayaa), in daabacaada lacagtan aaad looga xakameeyo. Bitcoin astaamaha ay leedahay ayaa iyana ka dhigay qaali iyo in dadku aaminaan isticmaalkeeda. Astaamaha waxa ka mida

  • Waa lacagtii ugu horraysay ee noocan ah ee la sameeyo.
  • Lama foojariyayn karo.
  • Xadigeedu waa u yaryahay, waxa jiri kara keliya 21 Million oo bitcoin ah, hadda 19.5 Million ayaa la soo saaray waxa hadhay wax aad u yar.
  • Blockchain-ka ay ku dhisan tahay oo security-giisu aad u adag yahay dadka tuugada ahna aanay jabsan karin.
  • In hab qarsoodi ah uu yahay, qofka diraya iyo ka loo dirayo aan la aqoonin keliya address-ka account-kooda uu ka muuqdo transaction sida address 18Yjk26aA oo u diray 4 Bitcoin address GKH78LoiV. Halka bangiyada caadiga labada qof xogtooda oo dhan la hayo.

Sababahaa dhawrkaa aya keenay in Bitcoin aad u faafto lana aamino. Bitcoin waxa loogu talo galay in sida lacagta caadiga loo isticmaalo sida in waxa lagu iibsado, biilasha lagu bixiyo iwm, hase ahaate hadda waxa ugu badan ee loo isticmaala waa asset ahaan in laga faaido oo la iibsado markay yartahay lana iibiyo markay badato maadama qiimaheedu aad isku bedbedelo. Waxa kale oo aad u isticmaala dadka iibiya waxayaabaha mamnuuca ah sida drugs-ka oo iska ilaalinaya in la ogaado cidda ay yihiin ama ay dawladdu daba gasho ama lacagta aan bangi dhigi karin maadaama oo aanay nadiif ahayn.

Blockchain

Blockchain waa tiknoolajiga uu ku dhisan yahay Bitcoin. Sida uu magacu sheegayo Block waa qurub ay ku jiraan macluumaadka transactions-kii dhacay. Chain waa hab silsiladeed oo block kasta ugu xidhan yahay block-ga ka horeeya. Block macluumaad nooce ah aya ku jira?. Marka uu qofku (sender) lacag bitcoin ah u dirayo qof kale (receiver) waxa uu transactionku maraa hab lagu hubinayo saxnaanshaha transactionkaas (verification process). Sida marka aad bangiga tagtid aad doonayso inaad lacag dirto waxa uu bangigu marka hore hubiyaa inaad xadigaa lacagataas ay ku jirto account-kaaga. Shaqadaa verification ee bitcoin ka waxa qabta dad loo yaqaan miners oo ah dadka leh computers-ka network-ga, adiga laftarkaagu waad noqon karta miner hadii aad haysatid computers awood leh iyo internet. Hadii transaction ku saxsan yahay waxa lagu geliyaa block-ga waxaana lagu xidhaa block-gii ka horeeyay si ay u sameeyan silsilad (chain). Markasta oo block cusub la sameeyo waxa uu software-ku abaalmarin bitcoin cusub ah uu siiyaa cidii block-gaa samaysay oo ah miners-ka, habka lagu sameeyo bitcoin cusub waa nooca. Halka dawladuhu markay lacag cusub doonayaan ay usoo daabacaan, bitcoin-ka cusub waxa reward loo siiya kolba dadka qabta shaqadaa verification-ka.
