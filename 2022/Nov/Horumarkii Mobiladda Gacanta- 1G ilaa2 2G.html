Horumarkii Mobiladda Gacanta- 1G ilaa 2G

Tiknoolajiyada mobiladu waxay soo martay jiilal kala duwan intii ay jirtay. Jiilashaa (Generations) oo loo soo gaabiyo xarafka G sida 1G, 2G, 3G, 4G iyo 5G, waxa ay ku kala duwan yihiin adeegyada iyo qaabdhismeedka network-ga. Mobile Phone ama Cell Phone sida meelaha qaar looga yaqaano waa aalad qofku sidan karo oo loo isticmaalo in ay dadku ku wada xidhiidhan cod (voice), qoraal (text) ama muuqaal (video) sida hadda jirta. Haddii aad rabto maqaalkan oo English ah fadlan riix halkan.

1G (First Generation)

Hadaba, 1G oo ahaa jiilka ugu horeeyay ee telefoonada gacanta lagu qaato waxa la hirgaliyay isticmaalkiisa 1980. Maadama oo bilaw ahayd tiknoolajiyadani, wadamo badan oo ku hawlanaa tiknoolajiyadani ayaa mid kastaaba iskii u samaystay. Wadamadaa Nordic ee yurubta waqooyi waxay samaysteen NMTs (Nordic Mobile Telephones), Uk Total Access Comunication System (TACS), sidoo kale U.S. Advance Mobile Phone System (AMPS). 1G waxa uu ahaa analogy system oo keena in tayada codku xumaado markasta oo masaafadu sii dheerato iyo in signalka uu ku bato waxa loo yaqaano interference. Dhibta kale uu laha ayaa waxa ka mida ma ay isticmaali karayn dad badani hal mar. Tusaale; hadii ay isku dayaan dad badani inay hal mar wicitaan sameeyaan ma ay u suurto geli karayn, sababta oo ah waxa xadidnaa janallada loogu talo galay in ay qaadan hadalka, dadka Frequency Division Multiple Access (FDMA). Ta kale, is ma ay geli karayn systems ka kala duwan ee wadan kasta u lahaa sida qofka UK jooga ee isticmaalaya TACS lama u xidhiidhi karayn ka U.S. jooga ee isticmaalaya AMPS.

2G (Second Generation)


Waxa hadaba muhiim noqotay si loo yareeyo dhibaatoyinka u lahaa 1G in la sameeyo jiilka labaad ee ama second generation (2G). Second generation waxa u xaliyay dhibaatoyin badan sida in uu kordhiyay tirada subscribers ka ee mar keliya isticmaali kara, in tayada codka uu fiicnaado iyada oo loo badalayo digital signal. Adeega fariimaha qoraalka ee Short Message Service (SMS) ayaa isna ka mid ahaa adeegyada uu 2G-gu soo kordhiyay. Waxa kale markii danbe lagu soo daray adeega loo yaqaano General Packet Radio System (GPRS) oo u saamaxay in lagu isticmaalo mobile-ka internet-ka markii ugu horraysay u soo kordhiyay adeegyo cusub.
Intaas oo faa’ido oo 2G-gu soo kordhiyay ayaa keentay in markiiba laga guuro system kii horee jiray ee 1G. Ka hore intii aan la hirgalin samaynta 2G-ga, Europe oo badanka hogaamiysay dhanka tiknoolajiyadda mobillada, ayaa isku raacday in ay sameeyan hay’ad u qaabilsan dejinta nidaamyada iyo protocols-ka uu lahaanayo jiilka labaad ee mobiladu halkii markii hore wadan kastaaba iskii u samysanayay. Hay’adan oo lagu magacaabo European Telecommunications Standards Institute (ETSI) waxa ay soo saartay Global System for Mobile Communication (GSM) oo ah hab qeexaaya sida uu shaqaynayo iyo astaamaha uu leeyahay 2G-gu. Taasi waxay suurto galisay adeega la yidhaa roaming-ka oo 1G suurto gal ahayn maadaama oo ay kala duwanaayeen hirarka (frequency) ay kala isticmaalayaan systemyadu, in uu noqdo faaido kale oo uu keenay GSM. Hase ahaate, North America iyadu waxa ay si horumarisay AMPS kana dhigtay 2G oo noqday D-AMPS. Inta kalee aduunka waxay wada qaateen GSM-ka.



Qaabdhismeedka GSM ka ee ay hay'adda ETSI samaysay waxa uu ka koobna saddex qaybood oo waawayn. Qaybta koowaad oo loo yaqaano Radio Access Network (RAN) oo qaabilsan isku xidhka mobile-ka iyo network-ga shirkadda kolba qofku SIM card-keeda isticmaalayo. Qaybta labaad waxa loo yaqaana Core Network-ga waana maskaxda networkga waxayna qaabilsantahay isku xidhka dadka is wacaya. qaybtani waxay ka kooban tahay MSC server oo qaabilsan isku xidhka iyo servers ay ku jiraan xogta macmiilka sida xogta shakhsiyadeed, macluumadka la xidhiidha billing-ka, goobta uu joogo macmiilku oo uu sheegaayo tower-ka kolba kuu dhaw iyo macluumaadka la xidhiidhi mobile-ka iyo SIM-ka sida IMSI, MSISDN, IMEI, TMSI iyo wixii la mida. Qaybta sadexaad waxa lo yaqaana Network Management System (NMS) oo qaabilsan ka warhaynta badqabka network sida marka ay cillad timaado in ay la ogaado halka ay cilladaasi ka jirto.

Mobilladii 1G iyo 2G-ga



Shirkadda Nokia waxa ay soo saartay mobile-keedii ugu horeeyay 1982, waxaana la odhan jiray Nokia Mobira Senator. Waxa uu culays ahaan gaadhayay ku dhawaaad 9Kg badankana waxa la saari jiray baabuurta. Mobile-kani waxa uu ku shaqaynayay 1G oo wakhtigaa billaw ahaa adeega kelliya ee loogu talo galaynaa uu ahaa voice-call.



Hase ahaate, shirkadda Motorolla ayaa soo saartay 1983 mobile ka yar ka Nokia oo la odhan jiray the DynaTAC oo qiima ahaan aad u qaali ahaa. Qiimahiisu waxa uu aha $3995 oo maanta u dhiganta ilaa $10300.



Markii la bilaabay isticmaalka 2G, waxa iyana bilaabmay samaynta mobillada ku shaqeeya adeegyada 2G-gu lahaa sida SMS iyo MMS. waxa so baxay mobillo leh screen yar oo fariimaha SMS-ka laga arki karo marka la akhrinayo iyo marka la qorayo. Nokia oo aad ugu horraysay so saarista teleefanada gacanta ayaa soo saartay mobile-keedi ugu horeeyay ee 2G ah waxaana la odhan jiray Nokia 3210.
